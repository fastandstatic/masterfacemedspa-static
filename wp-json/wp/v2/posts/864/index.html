We understand that some patients may be concerned about discomfort during the treatment, which is why we use a combination of topical lidocaine and a maxillary and mandibular nerve block with injected lidocaine to ensure a painless experience. The topical lidocaine is applied to the lips before the treatment to numb the area, while the nerve block is used during the procedure to provide additional pain relief.<\/p>\n\n\n\n
