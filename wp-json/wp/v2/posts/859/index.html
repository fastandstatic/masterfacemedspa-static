The Under Eye Correction procedure is a non-surgical solution that uses Restylane Lidocaine, a dermal filler based on hyaluronic acid, to correct dark circles and fine lines under the eyes. Hyaluronic acid is a naturally occurring substance in our body that helps maintain hydrated and youthful-looking skin. The innovative formulation with Lidocaine ensures that the procedure is virtually painless, allowing you to relax and enjoy a comfortable and pleasant experience.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Before starting the procedure, an anesthetic cream will be applied to the treatment area to ensure your comfort and reduce any possible discomfort. With the area numbed, Restylane Lidocaine will be precisely injected under the eyes. After the procedure, you can resume your daily activities almost immediately. As the hyaluronic acid integrates with your skin, you’ll notice how the dark circles and fine lines gradually fade away, revealing a rejuvenated and radiant look.<\/p>\n\n\n\n
